About Us

Who we are?

Swaroop Interior is a Mumbai‑based interior contractor and decoration specialist, founded in 2010. Located in Mumbai, the company provides professional services across residential and commercial interiors. Under the leadership of Mr. Swaroop Suthar, Swaroop Interior focuses on quality craftsmanship, timely execution, and innovative design solutions tailored to clients's needs.


Headed By Mr. Swaroop Jangid [Founder & M. D.], With A Strong Management Team, The Company Is Set To Transform The Interior Industry. The Company Manufactures & Distributes A Wide Range Of Interior Products From One Roof. With Successful Track Record & Vast Experience, We Provide A Reliable Support For All Your Interior Solutions. At Swaroop Interior, We Work With Client Instead Of Working For Them. This Ethos Of Partnering With Our Clients Has Helped Us To Build Some Of The Most Enduring Relationships In The Industry.


We Have A Dedicated Project Management Team for Our Interactions With The Corporate & Professional World. We Believe In Developing Customized Solutions For Differing Clients' Requirements & Also To Ensure Timely & Quality Execution. Being A Quality Conscious Organization, We Make Customer Satisfaction Our Top Priority & Focus On Brand Value & Employee Pride. We Provide Superior Quality Interior – Architectural Products Ensuring Quality Work.
